Once again, and in collaboration with other Libresoft researchers, we have finished the analysis of Lenny, the latest release of the Debian GNU/Linux distribution. As results, we have the presentation of Francisco Rivas at Debconf 2009, and the web interface to the database, published as usual in http://debian-counting.libresoft.es/, where you can find also analysis done to previous Debian releases. We are also writing a technical report which will be published here soon. Stay tuned ;-)
